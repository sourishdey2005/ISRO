Key Advantages

Why our dual-image approach outperforms traditional single-image super-resolution methods

Dual-Path Neural Network

Our specialized architecture processes both images simultaneously through separate encoder paths before fusing features in the decoder, preserving unique details from each source while combining complementary information.

Cross-Image Attention

Advanced attention mechanisms dynamically identify and combine the most valuable features from each image, with spatial and channel attention gates that learn to emphasize relevant regions and spectral bands.

ISRO-Optimized

Trained specifically on Indian satellite imagery characteristics and terrain features, with specialized loss functions that prioritize geographic accuracy over perceptual quality metrics.

Model Architecture

Custom Dual-Encoder U-Net with cross-attention gates and residual dense blocks. Features include:

  • Dual-path feature extraction
  • Multi-scale attention fusion
  • Perceptual + L1 composite loss
  • 4 upscaling capacity

Training Data

Proprietary dataset of 50,000+ image pairs from Cartosat, Resourcesat, and RISAT missions with ground truth HR images. Augmented with:

  • Atmospheric effects simulation
  • Sensor noise models
  • Geometric distortions
  • Indian terrain variations

Optimization

Quantized model with TensorRT acceleration delivers real-time performance:

  • 4 faster than baseline
  • 50% smaller footprint
  • Edge device compatible
  • FP16/INT8 support
